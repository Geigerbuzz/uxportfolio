Meet my heroes:

Dieter Rams

He is single handedly reponsible for bringing industrial minimalistic design to the masses, influincing the likes of Steve Jobs.

Horacio Pagani

Founder of Pagani Automotive, known for his artisan crafts and unmatched attention to detail and User experience.

Linus Torvalds

Over 70% of the internet (and my laptop) runs on the operating system he created as a college kid, GNU Linux.

Grace Hopper

Her genius created "COBOL", the first modern programming language and it is the backbone of our global banking system to this day.

Depending on your philosophical outlook you will either see me as a product of my influences or as a product of my predisposition at birth, I personally like Rene Descartes' perspective which combines the two ideals, in the end of the day no one can choose their birth circumstances.

However what you can choose is the people you surround yourself with, the art you digest, the science you enlighten yourself with and thus I think knowing who someone's heroes are is a useful tool to get an outline as to what sort of person they conduct themselves as, which tends to reflect both to their work ethic and their interpersonal skills.
